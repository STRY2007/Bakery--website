Welcome to Walnut Bakers, where every bite tells a story of love, passion, and tradition. Nestled in the vibrant city of Kanpur, our bakery is a haven for those who cherish the comforting aroma of freshly baked bread, the delicate sweetness of pastries, and the joy of indulgent cakes. At Walnut Bakers, we believe baking is more than just a craft—it's an art. Each of our creations is thoughtfully made from the finest ingredients, combining time-honored recipes with a touch of innovation. Whether it's a rustic loaf of sourdough, a buttery croissant, or a decadent celebration cake, every item reflects our commitment to quality and flavor. We pride ourselves on being a part of your cherished moments—be it a cozy breakfast, an afternoon treat, or a special occasion. Our friendly team is dedicated to making your experience delightful, ensuring you feel the warmth and care that goes into every bake. Thank you for making Walnut Bakers a part of Kanpur’s sweetest moments. We can’t wait to serve you!
